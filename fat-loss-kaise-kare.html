Fat Loss Kaise Kare? Complete Science-Based Guide

Fat loss ka matlab sirf weight kam karna nahi, balki body fat reduce karte hue muscle ko preserve karna hai. Iske liye calorie deficit, strength training, high-protein diet aur consistency sabse important factors hain.

Key Principles

  • 300–500 kcal calorie deficit
  • 1.6–2.2 g protein/kg body weight
  • 3–5 strength training sessions per week
  • Regular walking + cardio
  • 7–9 hours sleep

Common Mistakes

  • Crash dieting
  • Skipping protein
  • Only cardio
  • Poor sleep
  • Inconsistent routine
